# 3. 行列式展开定理

$$
D=a_{i,1}A_{i,1}+\dots+a_{i,n}A_{i,n}
$$
1. 范德蒙行列式
数学归纳法
$$
\begin{vmatrix}
1&1&\dots&1 \\
x_{1}&x_{2}&\dots&x_{n}\\  
\vdots \\
x_{1}^n&x_{2}^n&\dots &x_{n}^n
\end{vmatrix}=\begin{vmatrix}
1&1&\dots&1 \\
0&x_{2}-x_{1}&\dots&x_{n}-x_{1} \\
0&(x_{2}-x_{1})(x_{2}+x_{1})&\dots\\&&\dots
\end{vmatrix}=(x_{2}-x_{1})(x_{3}-x_{1})\cdots(x_{n}-x_{1})\begin{vmatrix}
1&1&\dots&1 \\
x_{2}&x_{3}&\dots&x_{n} \\
x_{2}^2&x_{3}^2&\dots x_{n}^2 \\
\vdots
\end{vmatrix}=\Pi_{1\leq j\leq i\leq n}(x_{i}-x_{j})
$$

# 4. 克莱姆法则

解线性方程组

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a_{1,1}&\dots \\
a_{1,2}&\dots \\ 
\vdots\\
\dots&a_{n,n}
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
x_{1}\\x_{2}\\\vdots\\x_{n}
\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}
b_{1}\\b_{2}\\\vdots\\b_{n}
\end{pmatrix}
$$
$$
x_{1}=\frac{D_{1}}{D}=\frac{\begin{vmatrix}
b_{1}&a_{1,2}&\dots\\b_{2}&a_{2,2}&\dots\\\dots
\end{vmatrix}}{\begin{vmatrix}
a_{1,1}&\dots &\dots\\
a_{1,2}&\dots \\ 
\vdots\\
\dots&&a_{n,n}
\end{vmatrix}}
$$
