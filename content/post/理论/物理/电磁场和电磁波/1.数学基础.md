### 1.1 矢量分析
#### 1. 标量和矢量
1. 定义 略过
2. 矢量用坐标分量表示
		$\vec{A}=\vec{e_{x}}A_{x}+\vec{e_{y}}A_{y}+\vec{e_{z}}A_{z}$
3. 矢量运算
	- 矢量的叉积
		$\vec{A} \times \vec{B} = \begin{vmatrix}\vec{e}_x & \vec{e}_y &  \vec{e}_z \\A_x & A_y & A_z\\ B_x & B_y & B_z\end{vmatrix}$	
4. 矢量的混合运算
	- 标量三重积  $\vec{A} \cdot (\vec{B} \times\vec{C}) = \vec{B} \cdot (\vec{C} \times \vec{A}) = \vec{C} \cdot (\vec{A} \times \vec{B})$
			是三个矢量构成的平行六面体的体积
	- 矢量三重积 
		- [[理论/物理/电磁场和电磁波/1.题型总结]]
#### 2. 坐标系
1. 直角坐标系	
	- 坐标位置矢量 $\vec{e_{x}},\vec{e_{y}},\vec{e_{z}}$  [[0b1370e8861acb4ff617242c84821d34_MD5.jpg|]]
![[0b1370e8861acb4ff617242c84821d34_MD5.jpg]]
	- **位置矢量** $\vec{r} = \vec{e}_x x + \vec{e}_y y + \vec{e}_z z$
	- **线元矢量** $\mathrm{d}\vec{r} = \vec{e}_x \mathrm{d}x + \vec{e}_y \mathrm{d}y + \vec{e}_z \mathrm{d}z$
	- **面元矢量**
		- $\begin{aligned}\mathrm{d}\vec{S} &= \mathrm{d}\vec{S}_x + \mathrm{d}\vec{S}_y + \mathrm{d}\vec{S}_z \\\mathrm{d}\vec{S}_x &= \vec{e}_x\mathrm{d}l_y \mathrm{d}l_z = \vec{e}_x \mathrm{d}y\mathrm{d}z \\\mathrm{d}\vec{S}_y &= \vec{e}_y \mathrm{d}l_x \mathrm{d}l_z = \vec{e}_y \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}z \\\mathrm{d}\vec{S}_z &= \vec{e}_z \mathrm{d}l_x \mathrm{d}l_y = \vec{e}_z \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\end{aligned}$
2. 圆柱坐标系
		$\begin{cases}x = \rho \cos\phi \\y = \rho \sin\phi \\z = z\end{cases}$
	- $\begin{cases}\vec{e}_\rho = \vec{e}_x \cos\phi + \vec{e}_y \sin\phi \\\vec{e}_\phi = -\vec{e}_x \sin\phi + \vec{e}_y \cos\phi\end{cases} \implies d\vec{e_{p}}=\vec{e_{\phi}}d\phi$
	- 线元矢量 $\begin{aligned}\mathrm{d}\vec{r} &= \mathrm{d}(\vec{e}_\rho \rho) + \vec{e}_z \mathrm{d}z \\&\downarrow \\\mathrm{d}\vec{r} &= \vec{e}_\rho \mathrm{d}\rho + \rho \mathrm{d}\vec{e}_\rho + \vec{e}_z \mathrm{d}z \\&\downarrow \quad \boxed{\mathrm{d}\vec{e}_\rho = \vec{e}_\phi \mathrm{d}\phi} \\\mathrm{d}\vec{r} &= \vec{e}_\rho \mathrm{d}\rho + \vec{e}_\phi \rho \mathrm{d}\phi + \vec{e}_z \mathrm{d}z\end{aligned}$
	- $\begin{cases}dl_\rho = d\rho \\dl_\phi = \rho d\phi \\dl_z = dz\end{cases}$
3. 球坐标系
	- $\begin{cases}dl_r = dr \\dl_\theta = r d\theta \\dl_\phi = r \sin\theta \, d\phi\end{cases}$
### 1.2 场
1. 方向导数
	$\frac{\partial u}{\partial l} \bigg|_{M_0} = \lim_{\Delta l \to 0} \frac{\Delta u}{\Delta l} = \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos\alpha + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\cos\beta + \frac{\partial u}{\partial z}\cos\gamma$
	场沿某个方向的空间变化率
2. 梯度
	$\operatorname{grad} u = \vec{e}_x \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \vec{e}_y \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + \vec{e}_z \frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = \nabla u$
	场在空间中的最大变化率
	- 一个标量场的性质可由其梯度唯一确定
3. 散度
	1. 通量 矢量场对一个闭合曲面的面积分 $\psi = \oint_S \vec{F} \cdot \mathrm{d}\vec{S} = \oint_S \vec{F} \cdot \vec{e}_n \,\mathrm{d}S$
	2. 散度 $\operatorname{div} \vec{F}(x, y, z) =\lim_{\Delta V \to 0} \frac{\oint_S \vec{F}(x, y, z) \cdot \mathrm{d}\vec{S}}{\Delta V}= \frac{\partial F_x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial F_y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial F_z}{\partial z} = \nabla \cdot \vec{F}(x, y, z)$
	3. 散度定理 $\oint_S \vec{F} \cdot \mathrm{d}\vec{S} = \int_V \nabla \cdot \vec{F} \,\mathrm{d}V$ 右式相当于对单位体积的矢量场的面积分求和，除表面以外的所有面的面积分会相互抵消
4. 旋度
	1. 环流 矢量场对一条闭合曲线的线积分 $\Gamma = \oint_C \vec{F}(x, y, z) \cdot \mathrm{d}\vec{l}$
	2. 旋度  
		1. 环流面密度 $\text{rot}_n \vec{F} = \lim_{\Delta S \to 0} \frac{\oint_C \vec{F} \cdot \mathrm{d}\vec{l}}{\Delta S}$
		2. 最大的环流面密度就是旋度 $\nabla \times \vec{F} = \begin{vmatrix}\vec{e}_x & \vec{e}_y & \vec{e}_z \\\frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\F_x & F_y & F_z\end{vmatrix}$
	3. 旋度定理
		$\oint_C \vec{F} \cdot \mathrm{d}\vec{l} = \int_S (\nabla \times \vec{F}) \cdot \mathrm{d}\vec{S}$ 右式相当于对单位面积的矢量场的线积分求和，除包围线以外的所有面的线积分会相互抵消
5. 旋度、散度关系
	- $\nabla \cdot (\nabla \times \vec{F}) \equiv 0$
	- $\nabla \times (\nabla u) \equiv 0$
6. 场的分类
	1. 无旋场
		仅由散度源产生的场 $\nabla \times \vec{F} \equiv 0$
	2. 无散场 $\nabla \cdot \vec{F} \equiv 0$
7. 亥姆霍兹定理
	若矢量场在无限空间中处处单值，且其导数连续有界，源分布在有限区域中，则当矢量场的散度及旋度给定后，该矢量场可表示为：**$$\vec{F}(\vec{r}) = -\nabla u(\vec{r}) + \nabla \times \vec{A}(\vec{r})$$
	- $u(\vec{r}) = \frac{1}{4\pi} \int_V \frac{\nabla' \cdot \vec{F}(\vec{r}')}{|\vec{r} - \vec{r}'|} \mathrm{d}V'$
	- $\vec{A}(\vec{r}) = \frac{1}{4\pi} \int_V \frac{\nabla' \times \vec{F}(\vec{r}')}{|\vec{r} - \vec{r}'|} \mathrm{d}V'$
